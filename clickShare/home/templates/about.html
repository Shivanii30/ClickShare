About us

Built to make sharing effortless

We understand that sharing files can be tedious. Swift Share makes it instant — upload once, share anywhere, no account required.

Secure by design

Each upload gets its own unique UUID-based link. No guessing, no accidental leaks.

Any file type

Documents, images, archives — we bundle everything into a neat ZIP automatically.

No account needed

Share with anyone, whether or not they have an account. Just send the link.

Our story

Swift Share started as a simple side project — frustrated by bloated, login-walled file sharing tools, we wanted something that just worked. Drop your files, get a link, done.

Today it handles multi-file uploads with automatic ZIP packaging, shareable download pages, and a clean UI that gets out of your way. Whether you're sending a report to a colleague or sharing photos with family across the world, Swift Share has you covered.
